Connection Status

Use the /slackify status command in Slack to see your current status.

Quick Commands

  • /slackify play - Resume playback
  • /slackify pause - Pause playback
  • /slackify status - View your status
  • /slackify enable - Enable auto-sync
  • /slackify disable - Disable auto-sync
  • /slackify sync - Manual sync

How It Works

Slackify automatically checks your Spotify playback every 10 seconds and updates your Slack status when the song changes.

Your OAuth tokens are encrypted and stored securely in MongoDB.
