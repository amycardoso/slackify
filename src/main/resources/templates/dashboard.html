Quick Commands

  • /trackify play Resume playback
  • /trackify pause Pause playback
  • /trackify status View your status
  • /trackify enable Enable auto-sync
  • /trackify disable Disable auto-sync
  • /trackify sync Manual sync

How It Works

Trackify automatically checks your Spotify playback every 10 seconds and updates your Slack status when the song changes.

Your OAuth tokens are encrypted and stored securely in MongoDB.
